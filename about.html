The dog (Canis familiaris or Canis lupus familiaris) is a domesticated descendant of
the wolf. Also called the domestic dog, it was selectively bred from an extinct population
of wolves during the Late Pleistocene by hunter-gatherers. The dog was the first species
to be domesticated by humans, over 14,000 years ago and before the development of agriculture.
